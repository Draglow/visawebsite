EthioGlobal Logo & Favicon

Professional logo and favicon design for EthioGlobal - Ethiopian Immigration Services. The design incorporates Ethiopian flag colors (green, yellow, red), passport/document imagery, airplane symbols for travel, and global connection elements that represent the website's mission of helping Ethiopians find opportunities abroad.

Design Elements Explained:

  • Ethiopian Flag Colors: Green (#10b981), Yellow (#FFD700), and Red (#EF4444) representing national identity
  • Passport/Document: White document with green elements symbolizing visa and immigration services
  • Airplane Symbol: Gold airplane representing international travel and opportunities abroad
  • Global Connection Lines: Curved lines showing connections between Ethiopia and the world
  • Ethiopian Star: Subtle star pattern referencing the national symbol
  • Color Harmony: Matches the website's premium gold (#D4AF37) and navy (#0F1B3C) palette
